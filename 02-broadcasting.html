Broadcasting rules

Given the arrays:

X = np.random.rand(10,3)
Y = np.random.rand(3)

which of the following will not produce an error:

  1. X + Y

  2. X[None, :] + Y

  3. X[np.newaxis, :] + Y

  4. X[None, :] + Y[:, None]

  5. X[:, np.newaxis] + Y

  6. X + Y[np.newaxis, :]

What will be the shapes of the final broadcasted arrays? Try to guess and then check using np.broadcast_arrays.
